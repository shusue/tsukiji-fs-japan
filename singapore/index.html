Who we are

Seafood expertise built at Yeonan Pier.

Established in 2022, TSUKIJI FS operates a seafood processing facility in Incheon. Fresh live fish are processed under a strict hygiene management system and supplied to retailers, restaurants, wedding halls and online channels.

Export coverage includes the United States, Canada, China, Vietnam, Thailand, the United Kingdom and other markets.
